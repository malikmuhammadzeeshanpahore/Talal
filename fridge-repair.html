Expert Fridge Maintenance

A broken refrigerator can lead to spoiled food and major inconvenience. We provide prompt, reliable refrigerator repair services across Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam to get your appliance cooling again.

From simple thermostat replacements to complex compressor overhauls, our experts handle it all with precision.

Our Fridge Services:

  • Compressor Repair & Replacement
  • Refrigerant Gas Leak Repair
  • Thermostat & Defrost System Fixing
  • Door Seal Replacement
  • Commercial Fridge Maintenance

Common Fridge Issues We Fix

  • Fridge Not Cooling: Can be due to a faulty compressor or thermostat.
  • Water Leaking: Usually a blocked defrost drain.
  • Ice Buildup: Faulty defrost timer or heater.
  • Loud Noises: Issues with the condenser fan or compressor.
