Expert Washing Machine Repair

A broken washing machine disrupts your daily routine. We provide prompt, reliable washing machine repair services across Jeddah to get your laundry routine back on track.

From drum issues and motor repairs to fixing electronic displays, our experts handle automatic and semi-automatic machines with precision.

Our Washing Machine Services:

  • Drum and Bearing Repair
  • Water Leak & Drainage Fixes
  • Motor & PCB Board Repair
  • Front-Load & Top-Load Services
  • Commercial Laundry Maintenance

Common Washing Machine Issues We Fix

  • Not Spinning/Draining: Can be due to a faulty pump or blocked filter.
  • Water Leaking: Usually a worn-out door seal or loose hose.
  • Loud Vibrations: Faulty shock absorbers or worn drum bearings.
  • Not Turning On: Electronic PCB board or timer issues.
