# Goal Description
The objective is to significantly enhance the homepage (`index.html`) layout to make it highly conversion-focused, more stylish, and include specific new sections requested by the user. The design will utilize advanced CSS styling (glassmorphism, gradient accents, hover micro-animations, and rich FontAwesome icons) to stand out against competitors in Jeddah.

## User Review Required
> [!NOTE]
> Please review the proposed new sections and the design direction below. If you want any specific text or pricing details included in the "Affordable Prices" section, let me know. Otherwise, I will use professional placeholder text highlighting budget-friendly services.

## Proposed Changes

### 1. Style & Animation Enhancements (`css/style.css`)
- **Animations:** Introduce new CSS keyframe animations (floating elements, smooth fade-ins, and icon bouncing).
- **Cards & Elements:** Upgrade existing cards (Why Choose Us, Process, Services) with deeper shadows on hover, glassmorphism backgrounds where applicable, and dynamic borders.
- **Icons:** Standardize and increase the size/impact of FontAwesome icons across all sections.

### 2. Homepage Structural Updates (`index.html`)

#### [MODIFY] `index.html`
- **Why Choose Us:** Upgrade the current layout to a more dynamic grid, adding glowing effects to the icons.
- **Process:** Change from simple numbered circles to a connected "timeline" or "step-by-step path" layout using CSS pseudo-elements for arrows/connections.
- **FAQ:** Enhance the existing accordion to have modern styling, perhaps with a split layout (image on one side, questions on the other).
- **[NEW SECTION] Affordable Prices:** Add a pricing/value section emphasizing no hidden fees, transparent quotes, and affordable AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts) packages.
- **[NEW SECTION] What Makes Us Special in Jeddah:** A localized section highlighting quick response times in Jeddah traffic, knowledge of local climate needs, and local testimonials or features.
- **[NEW SECTION] Location To Serve You:** Add a visual map/grid showing specific areas in Jeddah we serve (Al Safa, Al Rawdah, etc.) alongside Riyadh and Dammam, with a map embed or location icons.
- **[NEW SECTION] Quick Contact Form:** Insert an inline lead generation form directly on the homepage, likely just above the footer, to convert visitors instantly without them needing to click to the contact page.

## Verification Plan
1. **Visual Testing:** Verify that the new sections stack perfectly on mobile (responsive design) and do not overflow horizontally.
2. **Interactive Testing:** Ensure the new animations, FAQ accordions, and the new contact form display and function smoothly without breaking the layout.
